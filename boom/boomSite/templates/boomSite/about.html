About the Project

The project was carried out during the fourth semester of the Computer Technology Engineering degree at the Tecnol√≥gico de Monterrey, Mexico City. The objective was to create a video game with the theme of musical percussions here the user can interact and learn from music in a progressive way. During the class sessions we accomplish different cycles in order to get the final product. There were 4 cycles, in each one of them we developed different activities of the project. We started creating an idea with it's mockup to get a better concept of what we wanted to do. Then we created an static web page to gather all the information and establish the final design. Next we started building the videogame and implementing it's functionality. Finally we connect the databases and the videogame with the web page framework, in order to upload the page to a Azure cloud service using a virtual machine.

Boom

Meet Boom, our main character during this learning journey you are going to experience with Boom Percussion Game. Boom comes from Mars, a planet where there are no instruments. He was sent in an expedition to find the best way to make something the humans call music. with your help he will be able to lean about percussion instruments and identify them by their sound.
