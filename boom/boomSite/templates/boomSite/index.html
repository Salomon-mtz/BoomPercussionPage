About the Game

We've created a musical pedagogy game for gamers who want to learn about percussion instruments. In company of boom, our main character, you will be learning to identify by sound each of the different percussion instruments from different countries and cultures. The game has 4 levels, in each level your hearing and visual memory will be tested. In order to complete the level you must demonstrate a full knowledge of the previous one.

Objectives

Genre

This is a musical instruments puzzle game to test your ability to recognize percussion instruments through your hearing.

Stats

Real time global ranking and personal stats so you can measure your learning progress.

Jugability

This is a game for everyone, it is easy to play and guarantees constant learning.

Levels

Level 1

In the first level you will be tested with 3 different instruments. You can reproduce their sound in order to memorize it, once you are ready you can start the puzzle section.

Level 2

In the second level you will be tested with 5 different instruments. You can reproduce their sound in order to memorize it, once you are ready you can start the puzzle section.

Level 3

In the third level you will be tested with 7 different instruments. You can reproduce their sound in order to memorize it, once you are ready you can start the puzzle section..

Level 4

In the fourth level you will be tested with 10 different instruments. You can reproduce their sound in order to memorize it, once you are ready you can start the puzzle section.

How to Play

Step 1

Listen to each instrument sound by clcking in the speaker icon. Once you fill ready click the next button to start the quiz.

Step 2

Listen again de sound of the instrument by clicking the speaker icon. Try to match the sound to it's instrument image. Once you are ready click the check button to get your final score.

Step 3

If you got more than 5 points you will be able to pass to the next level. Otherwise, you will need to repeat the level in order to guarantee active learning.
